# C - Helical geometry is the primary geometric source of CISS rather than general chirality

## Summary

While the CISS acronym references "chiral," Foo et al. argue that helical geometry — continuous rotational pitch — is the operative structural feature that generates strong spin-orbit-mediated spin filtering. General chirality (mere lack of mirror symmetry) without helicity produces much weaker or no CISS in most models.
