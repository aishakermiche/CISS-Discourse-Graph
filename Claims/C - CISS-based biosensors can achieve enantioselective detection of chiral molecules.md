# C - CISS-based biosensors can achieve enantioselective detection of chiral molecules

## Summary

Because the CISS effect is sensitive to molecular handedness, devices that transduce spin-polarized electron currents into electrical signals can distinguish between enantiomers of chiral analytes. This provides a label-free, separation-free approach to enantioselective detection with potential pharmaceutical and biochemical applications.
