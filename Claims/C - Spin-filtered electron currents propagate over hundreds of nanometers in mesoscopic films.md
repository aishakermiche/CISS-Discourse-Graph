# C - Spin-filtered electron currents propagate over hundreds of nanometers in mesoscopic films

## Summary

In mesoscopic chiral organic films, spin-polarized electron currents generated by the CISS effect have been observed to persist over length scales of hundreds of nanometers — far exceeding the typical spin-coherence length of a few nanometers expected in single molecules. This suggests additional mechanisms (e.g., spin compensation during propagation) are at play.
