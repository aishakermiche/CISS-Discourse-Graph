# C - Spin-correlated radical pairs offer a tunable biomimetic platform for molecular quantum technologies

## Summary

Spin-correlated radical pairs (SCRPs) — transient two-spin systems with quantum-mechanically correlated spins — are sensitive to weak magnetic fields via the radical pair mechanism. Combined with CISS for spin initialization/readout, SCRPs offer a biomimetic, chemically tunable platform for quantum sensing and metrology that could complement solid-state spin systems.
