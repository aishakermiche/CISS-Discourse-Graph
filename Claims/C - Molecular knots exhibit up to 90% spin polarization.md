# C - Molecular knots exhibit up to 90% spin polarization

## Summary

Topologically chiral molecules in the form of molecular knots — which are chiral by topology rather than by helical geometry — have been experimentally shown to exhibit very high spin polarization (up to ~90%). This is among the highest reported CISS polarization values.
