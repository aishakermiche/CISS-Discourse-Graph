# E - Spin-filtered electron currents propagate over hundreds of nanometers in mesoscopic films

## Description

Experiments on mesoscopic chiral organic thin films reveal that spin-polarized currents generated via CISS persist over length scales of hundreds of nanometers. This is orders of magnitude larger than spin-coherence lengths expected from single-molecule spin-orbit coupling, indicating a collective or propagation-assisted mechanism.
