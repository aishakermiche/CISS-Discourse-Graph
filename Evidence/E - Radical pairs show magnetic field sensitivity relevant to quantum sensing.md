# E - Radical pairs show magnetic field sensitivity relevant to quantum sensing

## Description

Spin-correlated radical pairs (e.g., in flavin-tryptophan pairs in cryptochrome) exhibit singlet-triplet interconversion rates that depend on applied magnetic field strength via the radical pair mechanism. The resulting changes in chemical yield or fluorescence provide a measurable signal proportional to magnetic field, with demonstrated sensitivity relevant to Earth-strength fields (~50 μT).
