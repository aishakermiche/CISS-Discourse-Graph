# E - Molecular knots (non-helical topological chirality) show up to 90% spin polarization

## Description

Trefoil and other molecular knots — which are topologically chiral but not helical — have been synthesized and tested for CISS. They exhibit spin polarization values up to ~90%, among the highest ever reported for CISS systems. This challenges the hypothesis that helical geometry is *necessary* for strong CISS.
