# E - Spin polarization scales with helix pitch and length in model calculations

## Description

Theoretical models and supporting numerical simulations consistently show that the spin polarization generated by a chiral molecular wire increases with the number of helical turns and with an optimal pitch-to-radius ratio. This functional dependence on helical parameters supports the view that helicity — not merely chirality — is the operative geometric feature.
