# E - Chiral metals show CISS consistent with Onsager relations unlike organic systems

## Description

Measurements of CISS in chiral metallic structures (e.g., chiral plasmonic nanoparticles, chiral metal surfaces) show spin-selective transport that is consistent with Onsager-Casimir reciprocity — in contrast to organic biomolecular systems where reciprocity appears to be broken. This difference is attributed to the absence of disorder/impurity sites in pristine metals.
