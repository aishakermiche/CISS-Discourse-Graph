# E - Helical molecules such as DNA and peptides show robust CISS

## Description

Double-stranded DNA, alpha-helical peptides, and other biologically relevant helical structures have been consistently observed to act as spin filters in electron transmission experiments. Spin polarization values of 10–60% have been reported, and the effect is reproducible across multiple experimental platforms (photoelectron spectroscopy, magnetoresistance, electrochemistry).
