# E - Biomolecular CISS and solid-state CISS show mechanistically distinct signatures

## Description

Comparative analysis of CISS in biomolecular systems (DNA, peptides) versus solid-state chiral systems (perovskites, metals) reveals quantitative and qualitative differences in temperature dependence, polarization magnitude, voltage asymmetry, and field-reversal behavior that are difficult to reconcile within a single mechanistic picture.
