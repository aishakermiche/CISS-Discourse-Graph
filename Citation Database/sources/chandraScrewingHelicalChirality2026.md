abstract: "Helical chirality is an important, but underrated form of chirality than other types of chirality. Molecules with helical chirality impart a crucial role in several biological phenomena as well as in modern materials applications. Classically, the generation of chiral helicity in organic molecules relies on a ring extension by means of cycloaddition and related reactions. Recently, the peri -functionalization approach paved a new pathway for the generation of chiral helical molecules. In this article, we highlight the key advancements in these parallel approaches for the generation of helical chiral architectures."
