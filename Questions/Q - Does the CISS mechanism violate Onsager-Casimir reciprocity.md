# Q - Does the CISS mechanism violate Onsager-Casimir reciprocity?

## Context

Onsager-Casimir reciprocity relations constrain transport phenomena in time-reversal-symmetric systems. Some theoretical models of CISS require breaking this symmetry to produce finite spin polarization in the linear-response regime — an issue that has generated controversy and theoretical debate.
