# Q - What is the role of open quantum systems and dissipation in CISS?

## Context

Several theoretical models propose that CISS is fundamentally a non-equilibrium, dissipative phenomenon. Coupling of the chiral molecule to an environment (phonons, substrate, solvent) may be essential to generating the spin asymmetry, particularly in systems where intrinsic SOC is too weak to account for observed polarization.
