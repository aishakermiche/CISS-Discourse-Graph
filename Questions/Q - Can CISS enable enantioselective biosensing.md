# Q - Can CISS enable enantioselective biosensing?

## Context

CISS provides a transduction mechanism where molecular handedness (chirality) is converted to a measurable spin or electronic signal. This opens the possibility of detecting the enantiomeric composition of mixtures without traditional chromatographic separation.
