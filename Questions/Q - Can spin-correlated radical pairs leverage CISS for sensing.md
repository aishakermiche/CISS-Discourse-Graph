# Q - Can spin-correlated radical pairs leverage CISS for sensing?

## Context

Spin-correlated radical pairs (SCRPs) are transient paramagnetic species where two unpaired electron spins are quantum-mechanically correlated. Their singlet-triplet interconversion is sensitive to magnetic fields (the radical pair mechanism). CISS may initialize or read out spin states in SCRPs, enabling quantum magnetometry.
