# Q - Does CISS arise from helical geometry or more general chirality?

## Context

The "C" in CISS officially stands for "chiral," but it is debated whether the relevant geometric property is specifically *helical* structure or the more general property of *chirality* (lack of mirror symmetry). This has implications for which materials can exhibit CISS and how strong the effect can be.

## Answers (Claims)

- [[C - Helical geometry is the primary geometric source of CISS rather than general chirality]]

## Supporting Evidence

## Opposing Evidence
